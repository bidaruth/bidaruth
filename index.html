About Me

Hello. My name is Bida Ruth U. Magai, usually called Ruth. I come from Dogiyai Nabire Regency, Papua, the Mee tribe. I finished elementary school at SDN 1 Inpres Bomomani in 2019. I continued my schooling at grade 7 and 8 junior high school at, SMP St.Antonius Nabire, and 9th grade in SMP Sekolah Anak Indonesia, Bogor. I will finish junior high school in 2023. I continued high school at SMA Sekolah Anak Indonesia. And, finish high school in 2026. My hobbies are: Singing, playing guitar, and listening to music. At school I participate in more than 1 activity, namely: My sport is basketball. My art chooses vocals and music. My specialty is choosing public speaking. I also like writing poetry.
